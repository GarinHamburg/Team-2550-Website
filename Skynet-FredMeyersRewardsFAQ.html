Fred Meyer community rewards Q&A


    • How to do I link my Card?
      • 1) Create an online account with Fred Meyer.
      • 2) Link your existing Fred Meyer Rewards Card to your online account.
      • 3) Search by entering "OC Robotics Booster Club" or by using our non-profit number (87306).
      • If you are still having issues please contact us.
        •
    • What is Community Rewards?

        Community Rewards is a program run by Fred Meyer that allows you to link your Fred Meyer Rewards Card to a non-profit organization. Every time you make a purchase at your local Fred Meyer, they will donate a percentage to the organization you linked to. You can find out more here.

    • Do I still get my points?

        Yes! Linking your card DOES NOT effect how you get reward points, fuel points and rebates.

    • I don't have a Rewards Card. How do I get one?

        Applications for Rewards Cards are available at all registers or the customer service desk at any Fred Meyer store.

    • I don't see "Skynet" or "Team 2550" in the list of organizations.

        Our team is listed as "OC Robotics Booster Club". Sorry for any confusion this my have caused. You can also find us by searching for our program number. Our program Number is: "87306".
